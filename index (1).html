About Me

Hello, and welcome
to my space

My name is Sylwia Jarmul, and I am a Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique practitioner, lightworker, and guide for those ready to reconnect with the truth of who they are.

My path to this work has been anything but ordinary. For many years I carried deep questions about the meaning of life, the nature of the soul, and why we are here. There were times when I felt the weight of responsibility far too young — experiences that became the seeds for my own healing journey.

As I began to awaken spiritually, I realised that what I had once seen as hardship was also my greatest teacher. I learned that we are eternal, limitless souls experiencing life in a human body — and that we are the creators of our own reality.

While searching, I came across Dolores Cannon, whose insights into the New Earth and the spirit world stirred something profound within me. I felt an undeniable pull towards QHHT, knowing instinctively that this was the path I was meant to follow.

Today, I hold space for others with compassion, clarity, and a deep commitment to truth. My mission is simple: to help others remember who they truly are, reclaim their sovereignty, and live in alignment with their soul's highest path.
